# Deluxe Chaos Art Direction

Status: prototype-only, no-chain.

Deluxe Chaos expands Eternal Coil with meme-lore, Cardano culture, luxury flex, psychedelic humor, stake-pool houses, and satire while keeping the core image readable: a fractal Ouroboros serpent with a strong thumbnail silhouette.

## Principles

- Eternal Coil remains the visual anchor.
- Deluxe traits are spice layers, not the whole meal.
- Use transformed/parody-safe names and designs.
- Avoid direct protected brand names, exact logos, exact mascots, and real-person likenesses unless permission exists.
- Satire targets behavior and ecosystem tropes, not private individuals.
- Stoner and psychedelic traits stay playful and symbolic; avoid instructions or product promotion.
- Nested NFT traits use fake/prototype metadata only.

## Strongest First-Test Traits

1. Laser Oracle Eyes
2. Diamond Coil Grill
3. Myco Crown
4. Bone House Crown
5. Nested Mini Coil

These should be tested in 8-10 high-quality visual prototypes before expanding the active generator.

## Visual Density Guidance

- Maximum one major deluxe accessory per piece in early tests.
- Maximum one high-saturation effect plus one luxury/ice trait.
- Keep face/eyes visible unless the piece is intentionally mythic.
- Keep the tail-bite or coil-loop readable at 128px.

## IP / Brand Safety

Park exact names like luxury watch brands, other NFT project names, exact meme-token mascots, and real-person cameos. Convert them into original archetypes:

- Luxury watch -> Moonphase Drip Watch
- HOSKY aura -> Meme Hound Aura
- Big Joe cameo -> Cigar Don Coil
- Hype Skull -> Hype Relic Skull
- AstroBunz -> Astro Hopper Energy
- TROGG -> Cave Signal Energy
