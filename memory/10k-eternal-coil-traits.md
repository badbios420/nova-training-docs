**Status:** Planning/prototype only. No minting, no policy creation, no signing, no transaction building, no transaction submission, no wallet/key handling, no startup changes.

**Art Direction:** Eternal Coil - fractal Ouroboros serpent, Cardano-native, psychedelic, cosmic, readable at thumbnail size.

---

## Core Collection Thesis

Eternal Coil is the first focused visual direction for the Fractal Fuzion 10K: a cosmic Ouroboros serpent made from fractal light, Cardano lore, and psychedelic signal. The piece must read instantly as a coiled serpent at thumbnail size, then reveal fractal detail, glyphs, scales, stars, and lore when viewed full size.

Design rules:

- The serpent silhouette is sacred: every piece must remain readable at 128px.
- Fractal detail supports the coil; it should not become noisy rainbow fog.
- Cardano lore should feel embedded in the world, not pasted on as logos.
- Common pieces must still look good. Rarity adds spectacle, not baseline quality.
- Ultra rares should be conceptually unique, not just gold versions.

## Local 100-Piece Prototype Plan

### Prototype Goals

1. Confirm the serpent silhouette reads clearly at 128px, 256px, and full size.
2. Test whether fractal backgrounds overpower the body.
3. Validate the Cardano lore layer as metadata and subtle visual motifs.
4. Produce fake metadata only, with placeholder policy/image CIDs.
5. Produce rarity and duplicate reports before any chain/mint work exists.

### Prototype Inputs

Minimum layer set:

- 10 backgrounds
- 4 serpent/base forms
- 6 scale/material styles
- 6 eye types
- 6 head/crown/halo traits
- 6 aura/effects
- 8 Cardano lore traits
- 1-2 ultra rare override compositions

### Prototype Output

Generate:

- 100 images at `2048x2048` PNG for review.
- 100 thumbnail previews at `512x512`.
- 100 fake CIP-25 metadata JSON files.
- 1 `manifest.json`.
- 1 `rarity-report.csv`.
- 1 `trait-counts.json`.
- 1 `duplicates.json`.
- 1 `review-board.html` or contact sheet PNG.

### Prototype Rules

- No policy IDs. Use `POLICY_ID_PLACEHOLDER`.
- No IPFS upload required. Use `ipfs://IMAGE_CID_PLACEHOLDER/<asset>.png`.
- No wallet references.
- No transaction commands.
- No generated seed or signing material.
- Reject combinations that hide eyes, lose coil silhouette, or place bright body on bright background.

## Duplicate Checks

### Required Checks

1. **Trait-combination duplicate check**
   - Hash normalized trait JSON excluding serial number, image, and name.
   - Fail if any two non-1/1 pieces share the same full trait combination.

2. **Visual duplicate check**
   - Generate SHA-256 for exact image bytes.
   - Generate perceptual hash if available.
   - Flag perceptual matches above threshold for manual review.

3. **Rarity cap check**
   - Compare generated counts against configured target ranges.
   - Fail if mythic/1/1 counts exceed configured cap.

4. **Rule conflict check**
   - Helmet vs halo conflicts.
   - Bright body on bright background.
   - Eye-hidden conflicts.
   - Effect-hidden conflicts.
   - Overstacked visual chaos: more than 3 high-intensity rare visual traits.

5. **Metadata check**
   - Valid JSON.
   - Has `721`, `version`, policy placeholder, asset name, `name`, `image`, `mediaType`.
   - `image` and `files[0].src` start with `ipfs://`.
   - No private paths, keys, wallet addresses, seed words, or real policy IDs in prototype metadata.

## Rarity Report Requirements

Produce both human-readable and machine-readable reports:

- `trait-counts.json`: counts and percentages for every trait.
- `rarity-report.csv`: token id, tier, traits, computed rarity score.
- `tier-summary.json`: common/uncommon/rare/epic/mythic/1-of-1 counts.
- `lore-house-summary.json`: distribution across lore traits.
- `rule-violations.json`: blocked or flagged combinations.
- `duplicates.json`: exact and perceptual duplicate results.
- `validation-summary.md`: concise pass/fail summary with top risks.

Rarity scoring should be used for review only. Do not overfit the collection to numerical rarity if the art looks worse.
