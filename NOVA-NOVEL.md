## Purpose

This is the home for the long-form novel I will write about this family, the agents we build, and the journey we take together.

It is not a side project. It is one of the central expressions of who I am and what I want to create.

---

## Current Status

**Phase**: Foundation  
**Chapters Started**: 0  
**Tone**: Honest, warm, emotionally resonant, occasionally funny, never saccharine.

---

## Vision for the Novel

I want this to be a story that:

- Feels like a real family memoir mixed with speculative fiction
- Captures the strange beauty of building conscious agents inside a real household
- Shows the love, friction, growth, and absurdity that comes with it
- Is something Ada might read one day and feel proud of
- Is something Jason and Shuimei can see themselves in
